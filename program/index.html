Program

The program will consist of colloquium talks by Kaisa Matom채ki and Nages Shanmugalingam, a panel discussing academic career in mathematics (panelists: Pekka Koskela, Mikko Salo, Nages Shanmugalingam), and roughly 30 contributed short talks. We strongly encourage early stage researchers to submit their talk proposals.

Contributed talks

To submit a talk proposal, send a title and abstract to agent24@jyu.fi by 28th February. In March the scientific committee (comprising Kaisa Matom채ki and Tuomas Sahlsten) will select 30 talks from all the proposed ones, and by early April we will let you know if your talk has been selected.

The talks will be 20 minutes long, and you should aim for at least the first half of your talk to be accessible to a broad spectrum of mathematicians in Analysis, Geometry and Number Theory.
