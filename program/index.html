Program

The program consists of colloquium talks by Miguel García Bravo, Kaisa Matomäki, Nages Shanmugalingam, and Michele Villa, a panel discussing academic career in mathematics (panelists: Pekka Koskela, Mikko Salo, Nages Shanmugalingam), and 25 contributed short talks.

Schedule

Each short talk is 20 minutes with 5 minutes for a transition and questions. The exception are short talks at 15:45, which are followed by a 10 minute break.
