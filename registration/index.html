Deadlines

If you wish to give a talk, then the deadline for title + abstract submissions is 28th February, unless you are applying for a fee waiver (see information below). The talk proposals should be sent to agent24@jyu.fi.
The deadline for registration is 5th May.

Registration fee

The conference registration fee is 50€. In exceptional situations the fee may be waived, see information below.
If you wish to participate in the conference dinner, the dinner fee is 40€.
The payment is made via the registration form.

Fee waiver

The conference registration fee may be waived for participants who are selected to give a talk and who have no other available funding. If you wish to ask for a waiver, please send us a title + abstract for your talk proposal at agent24@jyu.fi by 28th February, and mention in your email that you ask for a waiver. In this case: DO NOT YET REGISTER. We will select the talks before the end of March. If your talk is selected, we will send you a notification, and you can eventually register (in early April) without paying the conference fee. Unfortunately, the dinner fee cannot be waived.
Note that this should be an exceptional situation: if you can cover the registration from a grant, please register normally at this point.
